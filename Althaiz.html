Why Are We Here?

All That Jazz is an established and experienced dance studio who throughout the years, has traveled all over the U.S. winning both regional and national dance titles.

At All That Jazz Dance Studio you will be treated as one of the family. Our main goal is to share our passion for dancing and music to people of all backgrounds and ages. We provide high quality social activities, instruction, and entertainment, all while striving to maintain an environment that is safe and welcoming.

We offer professional training in:

Ballet

> Tap

> Jazz

> Lyrical

> Pointe

Contemporary

> Modern

> Salsa

> Acro

> Hip-Hop
